About

Wei Pu received the B.S., M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in electrical engineering from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China, Chengdu, China in 2012, 2014 and 2018, respectively. He was a visiting researcher at Columbia University, New York, US, from 10/2017 – 10/2018. He was a recipient of the Newton Fellowship award in 2018. He is currently working as a researcher fellow at University College of London (UCL), London, UK, in the department of Electronic and Electrical Engineering.
